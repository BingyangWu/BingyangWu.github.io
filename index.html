Ph.D. Candidate

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science at Peking University, advised by Xin Jin. Before that, I received my B.S. degree (Summa Cum Laude) in computer science from Turing Class, Peking University. My research interests include machine learning systems, video conferencing, and cloud computing.
